Inauguration of NH Tower Project

Admin

January 1, 2024

Next Spaces Limited launched its latest project in Tejgaon, Dhaka this morning, marking the beginning of 2024. The new development is set to encompass 32 floors along with 5 basements. Notable figures such as Mr. Bimal Chandra Roy, Mr. Khalid Ahmed Khan, and Mr. Khondkar Nazmul Hassan attended the ceremony, actively participating in the proceedings. Ground improvement initiatives, including piling works, are scheduled to kick off on January 1, 2024.
