Signing ceremony Vistanext Ltd.

Admin

May 5, 2023

Vista Contracting and Investment Global Pte Ltd, wholly owned by Samsung C&T, has signed a joint venture agreement with Next Spaces Ltd. in Bangladesh. This company by the name of Vistanext Ltd. is intending to increase the local capacity in construction industry of Bangladesh by making full use of domestic resources through the introduction of modern and advanced technology. It will reduce Bangladesh's dependency on foreign expertise and resources.

On Thursday, May 4, Vistanext Ltd. was launched in Bangladesh by signing of an agreement between Samsung C&T and Next Spaces Ltd. Present at the signing ceremony was Mr. JW Kim, Executive Vice President of Vista Global, Mr. Abdul Muktadir, Chairman of Next Spaces Ltd. and chairman/MD of Incepta Pharmaceuticals Ltd., along with the other board of directors and senior officials.
