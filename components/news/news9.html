New equipment to the fleet!

Admin

May 27, 2024

Next Spaces Limited introduces four brand new BAUER GB-80S and six DHG V/SF hydraulic diaphragm wall grab machines to their fleet. This addition will enable the company to expand its capabilities to execute projects that require such advanced technology. These machines can construct diaphragm walls (D-Wall) up to 80 meters deep and 1500mm wide.
