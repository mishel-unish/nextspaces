Inauguration of Emnipur Project

Admin

August 29, 2023

In Tejgaon, Dhaka, Next Spaces Limited has commenced the construction of a 32-story commercial building featuring seven basements. At the groundbreaking ceremony, notable figures such as Mr. Abdul Muktadir, Mrs. Akther Jahan Hasneen Muktadir, Mr. Khalid Ahmed Khan, and Mr. Khondkar Nazmul Hassan were present to witness this significant milestone. Their collective involvement underscores the start of a transformative project that aims to contribute to the area's urban development. The forthcoming structure is anticipated to serve as a dynamic hub for commerce, reflecting the forward-thinking vision of those involved in this exciting venture.
